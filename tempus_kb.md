# Tempus Oncology Portfolio & Performance Metrics

## 1. Tempus xT (Next-Generation Sequencing)
- **Type:** Targeted DNA (648 genes) + Whole Transcriptome RNA sequencing.
- **Turnaround Time (TAT):** Industry-leading 9-11 calendar days from receipt of sample. 
- **Key Differentiator:** RNA sequencing co-amplification significantly increases fusion detection rates compared to DNA-only competitor panels.

## 2. Tempus xF (Liquid Biopsy)
- **Type:** Cell-free DNA (cfDNA) circulating tumor DNA assay (105 genes).
- **Turnaround Time (TAT):** 6-8 calendar days from receipt.
- **Key Differentiator:** Solves the "tissue scarcity" issue. Ideal for advanced NSCLC, colorectal, and breast cancer patients when solid biopsy tissue is unavailable or insufficient.

## 3. The Tempus ONE Clinical Assistant
- **Description:** Voice and text-activated AI interface allowing oncologists to instantly query test results and matching clinical trials directly from the clinic floor.
